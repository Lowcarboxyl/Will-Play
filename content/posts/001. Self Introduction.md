You know that guy who walks up to the front of a kinda sleepy local restaurant and asks to speak to the manager? Not out of anger or a desire to fight, but just so he can ask the owner questions about how they choose their food, or to offer some compliments and thoughts? Well, maybe that’s not a common type of guy. Anyway, that’s me.

My brain is always filled with thoughts and arguments. Instead of brooding on them for hours or years, I’ve decided to put them down on digital paper and exercise the most human action of all: taking the grand sum of my experiences and translating an idea from my head to other humans. 

Speaking of my brain, it is intensely systems-focused; I love thinking about how things work or how they reached their current state. In that same vein, I’m constantly analyzing the spaces where we live, the history of how we arrived at this point in our lives, the ways technology advances, and the games and media that offer the best way to explore it all.

I’m a firm believer that we are the sum of the systems we interact with. This blog is my attempt to document that interaction.

In that same spirit, if anything I say makes you think, or if you just want to say hi, please reach out. It doesn’t have to be a deep conversation; a simple "hi" and a thought you had is enough. In this busy world, knowing that we can still connect, rather than just lurking forever, is good enough for me.

I was actually crashing out at the time. After seeing Japan, reserving outdoor tourism is second nature now, but at the time, this was one of four national parks that implemented this system to deal with crowds. There's a lot to be said about this, but long story short, it was implemented in one of the worst ways possible. I actually needed some time to just walk by the river, think through the reasons why they would do this, and do tons of googling to find out who decided this—the whole deal.
